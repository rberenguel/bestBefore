Here you can see the expiries of all tabs currently stored, as well as delete any expiry that may look weird. So, the red cross won't delete a tab, but the expiry date related to it. Tabs present in the expiry list internally but not in Chrome have been styled as `display: none`, removing this styling will let you interact with those (mostly used for debugging).

When tabs are more than 6h close to expiry they will show in increasingly alarming shades of red.

By clicking on the tab id button you can switch to that specific tab. If the tab is in another window though, window focus won't change.
